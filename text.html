New york

New York City, located at the southern tip of New York state, is the most densely populated major city in the United States, with a population of 8,804,190 in 2020. It is the geographical and demographic center of both the Northeast megalopolis and the New York metropolitan area, which is the largest metropolitan area in the US by both population and urban area. With a larger population than 38 of the nation's 50 states, New York City is more than twice as populous as Los Angeles.
